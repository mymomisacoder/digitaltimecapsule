New Year Resolution

To welcome the new year, I will be making the following resolutions:

  • Spend more quality time with family and friends.
  • Start journaling to reflect on daily activities and thoughts.

  • Explore a new hobby, like painting or playing a musical instrument.

  • Volunteer for community service at least once a month.
  • Travel to at least one new destination and experience a different culture.
